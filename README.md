## ğŸ“– What Is GDPDI?

The **Global Durga Puja Digital Index** is the world's first structured, standards-based ranking of Durga Puja association websites across all nations.

**Durga Puja is a UNESCO-inscribed Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity** (December 2021). Celebrated across five continents by the global Bengali diaspora, the festival's digital presence had never been systematically evaluated â€” until now.

Every association website is scored against **11 internationally accepted criteria** using frameworks from W3C, Nielsen Norman Group, Google, and Gartner. Scores are transparent, reproducible, and independently verifiable.
